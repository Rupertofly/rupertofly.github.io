Project Summary

Purpose of Project

cosm.os was my industrial design honours project. it was born out of a mix of my fascination with systems thinking, and my difficulty reconciling the positive community aspects of social media and the negative aspects

Objective

cosm.os Aim was to change how we look at social media, by extracting all of the human context leaving and abstract system, that placing that system into an interactive context so that it can be experimented with and observed.
